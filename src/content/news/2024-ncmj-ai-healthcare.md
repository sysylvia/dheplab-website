Sean Sylvia and Junier Oliva co-authored "Artificial Intelligence in Health Care: Opportunities, Challenges, and the Road Ahead" in the North Carolina Medical Journal (Vol 85, Issue 4, 2024).

The article addresses navigating the challenges of bias, privacy, and ethical considerations in AI healthcare applications. As guest editor for this special issue on AI in healthcare, Dr. Sylvia worked with leading experts to explore practical implementation challenges and opportunities for responsible AI adoption in clinical settings.

Key themes include:
- The need for robust frameworks for AI evaluation
- Continuous learning and model updating
- A commitment to equity in AI deployment
- Balancing innovation with responsibility

The article emphasizes that ensuring AI can deliver on its promise to enhance outcomes requires collaborative approaches across disciplines and institutions.
