We're excited to launch DHEPLab Insights, a space for sharing our research, perspectives on digital health policy, and updates from the lab.

## What to Expect

Here you'll find:

- **Research Updates**: Summaries of our latest papers and ongoing projects
- **Policy Analysis**: Commentary on digital health regulation, reimbursement, and implementation
- **Methodological Insights**: Deep dives into our approaches to evaluating clinical AI and value-based care
- **Lab News**: Team updates, conference presentations, and collaboration announcements

## Stay Connected

Subscribe to our newsletter for monthly digests of new content, or follow us on [Twitter](https://twitter.com/dheplab) for real-time updates.

We welcome feedback and collaboration opportunities. [Get in touch](/contact) if you'd like to learn more about our work.
