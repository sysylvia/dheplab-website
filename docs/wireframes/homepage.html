What We Study

Research Areas

Clinical AI Evaluation

Rigorous assessment of AI-assisted clinical decision support systems, studying human-AI collaboration and appropriate reliance in healthcare settings.

Value-Based Care

Economic analysis of alternative payment models and their impact on healthcare quality, cost, and provider behavior in value-based arrangements.

Digital Health Policy

Examining regulatory frameworks, implementation strategies, and policy levers that shape the adoption and effectiveness of digital health technologies.
